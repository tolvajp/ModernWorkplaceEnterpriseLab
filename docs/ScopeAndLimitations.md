# Scope and Assumptions

## Scope

This lab focuses on:

- Microsoft Entra ID identity and access design
- Conditional Access at the model level
- PowerShell automation enforcing decisions
- Decision traceability and current-state modeling

## Explicitly out of scope

- Production-scale operations
- Multi-tenant management
- Legacy on-prem AD integration
- Session-level or continuous access enforcement

## Assumptions

- Single-tenant lab environment
- Full administrative control
- Decisions are made by a single engineer
- Security-first defaults unless stated otherwise
